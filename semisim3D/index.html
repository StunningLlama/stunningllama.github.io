Brandon's Electromagnetic Simulation 3D

Make your own three-dimensional circuits. Some features of my program include:

  • Interactive circuit drawing
  • Visualization of electromagnetic fields
  • Different materials (conductors, dielectrics, ferromagnets, etc.)
